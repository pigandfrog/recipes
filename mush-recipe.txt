For this recipe you will need two quarts of water and two 
cups of ground wheat

1.	Put water into pot and heat
2. 	Add about 1 teaspoon of salt.
3. 	When water is boiling add the two cups of wheat.
4. 	Turn the burner tempurature to low and stir every
	three to four minutes.
5. 	When the cereal has thickened it is ready to eat!
